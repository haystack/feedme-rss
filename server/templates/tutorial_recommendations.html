Step 6/8

FeedMe learns who you tend to recommend certain items to, and recommends those people to you when you read similar items in your subscriptions.

After using FeedMe for some time, you will get a list of recommened recipients of an item you are reading:

To see more recommendations, you can click the "more..." link:

Recommendations are only suggestions. If you like some of the recommended recipients, click on them to select them for a message:

If you click on a recommendation a second time, you will de-select them. If you are not happy with the recommendations FeedMe gives you, feel free to type the person's email address into the text box like you did in the last section.

To share with a recommended person, click on their name to select them, then click Now or Later to send the e-mail.

Now that you're a FeedMe Pro, you may proceed to the next step.
