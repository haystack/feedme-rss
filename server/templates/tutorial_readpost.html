Now that you are logged in, we are ready to use FeedMe!

Open an item in one of your Google Reader subscriptions. Under the title of the item you are looking at, you will see something new: FeedMe!

When you first start using FeedMe, it won't know who you like to share with. So, you'll need to add the first few people yourself. Next to the FeedMe logo, you will see a text box. This is a text box in which you can type email addresses of people you know:

Type in your own email address, and press enter. After that, type 'feedme@csail.mit.edu' and press enter. You will see something like this:

The above image means that 'marcua@csail.mit.edu' [your email address] and 'feedme@csail.mit.edu' will be receiving an email from you with this post. Underneath each email address, you will get a message such as "Saw it Already" if the person has seen this item already, or "Received 5 today" if the person has received 5 FeedMe emails today already.

You are now ready to send the message. You can type an optional message to send to the recipients, and then click "Now" to send the message now:

Check your email! You should have just received the message!

You also see a "Later" button. If you do not want to send the message to the person right now (perhaps you have sent them many messages today, and do not want to burden them), you can push "Later" instead of "Now." Every few days, we will send the recipient an email with all of the items that were shared with them "Later."

Once you're comfortable with FeedMe, you may proceed to the next step.
